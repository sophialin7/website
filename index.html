About Me

Hi, I am Sophia Lin and I am a junior at Newport High School with a strong interest in computer science and aeronautical engineering.

In the summer of 2019, I explored aviation and aerospace at Boeing Academy, where I built a base of fundamental aeronautical knowledge in preparation for the FAA Private Pilot Exam. I also trained in the ALC (Aviation Learning Center) simulators weekly and applied learning to collaborative team projects.

In my sophomore year, I started STEM League, a club dedicated to STEM outreach and exploration for high school peers. As the club leader and president, I delegate officers meeting and work with local librarians and staff to plan STEM League events in the Bellevue community, specifically targeting K-12 students.
